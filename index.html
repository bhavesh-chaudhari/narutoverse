The Naruto Universe

Backstory

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

His birth took place in a village called konoha(village hidden in the leaves). Throughout the naruto series we see him learning different jutsu and work really hard in order to become the hokage of Konoha.

Naruto was born during a time of relative peace in an era of world history known as the "Shinobi World". The Shinobi World is separated by the division of five great Shinobi countries: powerful nations possessing powerful militaries.

Wars are often fought between these five great nations but the world has no conventional weapons of war, as we would understand them. Instead battles are fought between ninjas, who are trained from a young age to harness their life force (or "chakra") to create attacks and illusions. Chakra is the driving force behind the whole naruto plot.

All The five nations in the shinobi world are led by governmental leaders known as daimyō, who are in charge of ruling their individual nations solely through a monarch-like government. These countries are divided equally on an unknown continent to create balance in the Shinobi world. They are the Land of Earth in the Northwest, the Land of Lightning in the Northeast, the Land of Water in the East, the Land of Wind in the Southwest, and the Land of Fire in the centre.

The countries are named after the dominant chakra nature of each of the areas. Each nation has ninja villages and each village have a kage(the highest representative of a village). Sounds interesting ! doesn't it ?

Overall, naruto is one of the most epic animes ever created and has a huge fanbase. The anime has a total of 720 episodes and watching all of them is totally worth it(but skipping fillers is also a choice). What happens eventually in the series is upto you to find out. But i would say one thing that naruto becomes powerful and powerful means not only by his justu but also by his ability to understand and care about people.

Naruto has got epic plot, love, emotions, fights, friendships, betrayals and what not ! So, this was a bit of the backstory and if you haven't watched it yet then i would highly recommend you to do so. Now here lies the end of this backstory before you get the question "Can he beat goku though ?"

But what is this website ?

This website is dedicated for naruto fans. It aims to gradually create a mini naruto world in form of a website. Here, one can find details about villages, characters, quotes, narutoverse imagery, different jutsus in naruto, etc. The project is going to take place gradually and the development will be a continuous process.
